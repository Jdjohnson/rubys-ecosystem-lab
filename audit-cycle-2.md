## Critical (must fix)
- [src/data/food-chains.ts:19-24, src/data/scenarios.ts:5-29, src/data/scenarios.ts:89-113, src/data/habitats.ts:31-56, src/data/organisms.ts:196-235] The habitat contradictions from cycle 1 are still in the curriculum. ## Observations
- Several cycle-1 fixes did land cleanly: TapToClassify no longer reveals answers up front, CardSort misses no longer blurt out categories, the hydration/build issues are gone, restart affordances exist, and zoom / reduced-motion support is in much better shape.
- The biggest remaining pattern is not broken code; it is curriculum trust. The rough spots now are mostly unfair quiz scope, lingering science contradictions, and exploration screens whose instructions still do not match their completion rules.
- The strongest regression from the answer-hiding work is in Habitat Explorer: hiding classification by shortening labels was the right instinct, but trimming to the last word overshot and made some cards less understandable.
- Visually the app now feels more coherent than cycle 1, but the food web and habitat modules still feel like they belong to a rougher sub-family than the rest of the app because their rules and labels are less clean.
- Verification check: `npm run lint` and `npm run build` both pass on the current tree.
